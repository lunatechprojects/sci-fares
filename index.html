Albert Einstein

Physicist and Scientist

The Scientist who hypothetically stated that energy is equal to mass. Energy Mass equivalence that is E=mc2 where E is energy in joules,m is mass in Kg and c is speed of light that is 3x108ms-2
Alternatively, this equation can be used by using the following units

  • E in MeV
  • m in MeVc-2

    • Mass can be converted from kg to atomic mass unit(u) by dividing by 1.661 X 10-27
    It can also be converted from u to MeVc -2 by multiplying by 931.5
    This form of mass and energy conservation and conversion is commonly observed in nuclear reactions and situations where high speed particles collide
